Rule Application
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Transformation rules (reaction patterns) can be applied to graphs
(molecules) to create new graphs (molecules). The transformations
(reactions) implicitly form a directed (multi-)hypergraph (chemical
reaction network).
